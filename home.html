BIRIYANI

Biryani (/ bɜːrˈjɑːni /) is a mixed rice dish originating among the Muslims of South Asia.

It is made with Indian spices, vegetables, rice, and usually some type of meat (chicken, goat, lamb, prawn, and fish), or in some cases without any meat, and sometimes, in addition, eggs and potatoes..

POORI

Puri (sometimes spelled as poori) is a deep-fried bread made from unleavened whole-wheat flour that originated in the Indian subcontinent. It is eaten for breakfast or as a snack or light meal. It is usually served with a savory curry or bhaji, as in puri bhaji, but may also be eaten with sweet dishes.

Puri can be eaten with many savory accompaniments, including korma, chana masala, dal, potato-based curries (for example, saagu, bhaji, bhujia, Aloo ki tarkari, shaak, and sambharo), shrikhand and basundi.
